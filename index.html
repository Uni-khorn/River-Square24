Welcome to River Square 24

Located in the heart of Phnom Penh, just steps away from the Royal Palace and historic Raffles Hotel, River Square 24 offers a dining experience like no other. Our chef combines traditional Khmer recipes with contemporary techniques to create dishes that celebrate Cambodia's rich culinary heritage while embracing global influences.

Fresh Local Ingredients

We source our ingredients from local farms and markets, ensuring the freshest produce and sustainable seafood.

Expert Chefs

Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Sokha, brings over 15 years of international experience.

Curated Wine Selection

An extensive collection of wines from around the world, perfectly paired with our menu.
